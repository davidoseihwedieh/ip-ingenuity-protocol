How IP Tokenization Works

Transform your intellectual property into tradeable digital assets in just four simple steps.

1

Submit Your IP

Upload your intellectual property documentation including patents, copyrights, trademarks, or creative works with supporting legal documents.

2

Legal Review & Valuation

Our expert team conducts thorough legal verification and AI-powered market valuation to determine the optimal tokenization structure.

3

Token Creation

Smart contracts are deployed on the blockchain, creating secure, compliant tokens that represent fractional ownership of your IP assets.

4

Trade & Monetize

Your tokenized IP goes live on our marketplace where investors can buy, sell, and trade while you receive automatic royalty distributions.
